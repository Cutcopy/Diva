LinkedIn Diva

Attention Sales and Business Professionals:

Learn Why You Need LinkedIn Even If You Don’t Think You Do

Here are 8 Reasons You Need a Great LinkedIn Profile:

  1. LinkedIn has over 275 million member with 2 new members joining every second. LinkedIn is the world’s largest professional network. It is assumed that most professionals have a LinkedIn profile. One out of 3 people on the planet have a LinkedIn profile. The average CEO has 930 connections. 41% of millionaires use LinkedIn. LinkedIn the most obvious way to identify influential individuals at specific organizations With over two million prospects, brand conscious leaders know the power of LinkedIn and have a presence

  2. LinkedIn shows up at the top of Google searches.  Studies have shown that over 60% of the time that your name is referred, you will be “Googled”. When someone googles you, your LinkedIn profile will likely show up in the first or second spot –This is important because, according to a Gravitate Online study of over 8 million clicks, more than 94% of users clicked on the first page results while fewer than 6% clicked to the second page. The first and second positions get 50% of all clicks

  3. You will be researched by prospects, clients, competitors and referral partners and you rarely get a second chance to make a positive first impression. LinkedIn gives you an opportunity to control what the people who are researching you discover about your strengths and, more important, about your brand. They search for you before meeting you for the first time, or after reading an article that you were featured in, or when deciding whether to respond to your email.

  4. LinkedIn is packed with valuable resources. Whether you are researching what your competitors are doing or want an introduction or want to know who is in a prospective client’s network, LinkedIn is the place to be. LinkedIn can help you open doors to new clients, find service providers, make connections or help you demonstrate your credibility with recommendations and testimonials.

  5. LinkedIn allows you to stay connected. In a world where we’re all overwhelmed with more tasks than time, staying on the radar of our ever-growing group of contacts is a challenge. LinkedIn allows you to update your status regularly, instantly letting everyone in your audience know what’s new in your world and where to find you. You can expand this audience easily by joining groups, which give you added opportunities to post your thoughts and comment on others’ posts.

  6. Your LinkedIn profile is the foundation to your professional presence and your branding strategy. Your profile represents you, your public image and your marketing message. LinkedIn allows you to create your branding platform. Using images, video and photos, you can create a summary that is a three dimensional view of your brand to paint a compelling picture of who you are in the world.

  7. LinkedIn helps you stay current. Following thought-leaders and companies helps you stay on top of what’s happening in your industry or job function – firsthand info that is unfiltered by the media. Through brand association, you make a statement about yourself whenever you follow a company or leader. Those names influence the people who are viewing your profile.

  8. LinkedIn gives you a platform to publish and share your expertise. The Pulse platform of LinkedIn is one of the best ways to create followers and demonstrate your knowledge and expertise.
